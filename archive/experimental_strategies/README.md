# Experimental Strategies Archive

Strategies tested but not deployed. Kept for future reference.

## Low Volatility Factor (2024-12-15)

**Strategy:** Buy 50 lowest volatility S&P 500 stocks (3-year weekly volatility)

**Results (2016-2024):**
- CAGR: 9.07% vs SPY 14.49%
- Drawdown: -31.6% vs SPY -33.7%
- Sharpe: 0.71 vs SPY 0.81

**Why not deployed:** Underperforms in bull markets. Risk reduction minimal.

---

## Asset Class Trend Following (2024-12-15)

**Strategy:** Hold 5 ETFs (SPY, EFA, BND, VNQ, GSG) when above 10-month SMA

**Results (2015-2024):**
- CAGR: 3.4% vs SPY 13.4%
- Designed for crash protection, not outperformance

**Why not deployed:** Significant underperformance in bull market. Commodities/bonds drag.
